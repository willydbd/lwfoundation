We are nonprofit team
based in Abuja

Loveth Williams Save A Life Foundation is nonprofit organisation founded by beauty queen, Loveth Williams (Miss Ambassador for Peace Diaspora 2013), registered under the nonprofit act 71 of 1997, it was founded with the philosophy "SAVE A LIFE" this entails doing anything humanly possible to render necessary assistance to the society left out, weaks.

We give children, widows, handicaps, vulnerables in Nigeria what everyone of them deserves- a healthy start, the opportunity to learn and protection from harm. When disaster strikes, we put their need first. We advocate for and achieve large-scale change for them. We save their lives
